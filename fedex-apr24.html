Next moves · two small inputs Decision lives with Herb.

Two asks. Both tighten the pilot.

Neither is an expansion ask. Both are control mechanisms that make the next read cleaner, faster, and more defensible internally.

Ask 01 · Control

Daily SFTP feed of paid & closed invoices.

Read-only. Once a debtor tells us they already paid, we need to confirm in hours not days. This suppresses resolved accounts automatically, improves reporting accuracy, and stops us asking a customer twice. Integration already built, live on 3 other enterprise accounts.

Ask 02 · Learning

A 1,000-account cohort from the last 30 days of billing.

Batches #1 and #2 are legacy aged AR. A fresh-aging cohort would let us compare the engine's behavior on newer invoices — where the invoice is still recognized and the contact is still fresh. Same KPIs, 10-day read, published side-by-side.
