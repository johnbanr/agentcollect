Frequently asked questions

What are the success-only fees?
Our fees are a percentage of the amount we recover for you. You pay nothing upfront and nothing if we don't recover. Specific rates depend on your volume, invoice age, and industry. Book a demo to get a custom quote.
How is this different from a collection agency?
Traditional agencies take 25-50% commissions, damage your brand, and take months to act. AgentCollect uses AI agents that contact your customers within minutes, under your brand, at a fraction of the cost. Licensed attorneys handle escalation when needed.
What's the difference between first party and third party?
First party collection means AI agents reach out under your brand name. Your customers see your company, not a collection agency. Third party collection involves a licensed attorney sending demand letters and escalating on your behalf when first party outreach is exhausted.
Is there a minimum volume or contract?
No minimums and no long-term contracts. Start with a single invoice or your entire portfolio. Cancel anytime. Enterprise accounts ($1M+/month) get custom pricing with no license fees.
How long does setup take?
Most customers are live within 24 hours. Upload a CSV or connect via API. We handle phone number provisioning, compliance configuration, and brand customization.
Is this compliant with FDCPA and Regulation F?
Yes. Every outreach follows FDCPA, Regulation F, TCPA, and state-specific regulations. Call timing, frequency limits, opt-out handling, and required disclosures are all built into the platform. We're SOC 2 Type II certified.
What does the free pilot include?
Upload a batch of invoices and let our AI agents work them for 30 days. You pay nothing upfront. If we recover money, you pay the agreed success fee. If we don't recover anything, you pay nothing. Zero risk.
