The Real Difference

Enterprise suite vs. autonomous recovery

HighRadius is the most comprehensive AR platform in the enterprise market. Cash application, deductions management, collections worklists, credit scoring — it covers the full order-to-cash cycle with deep ERP integrations. For a Fortune 500 CFO managing a large AR team across multiple ERPs, HighRadius is the standard choice.

The limitation is that HighRadius is a tool, not a service. Its collections module creates prioritized worklists and automates email sequences, but someone on your team still has to make the calls, negotiate payment plans, and escalate to legal. If your collectors are maxed out — or if you don't have enough of them — HighRadius optimizes a bottleneck rather than removing it.

AgentCollect takes the opposite approach: fully autonomous recovery. Each overdue account gets its own AI agent that researches the company, identifies the decision-maker, calls them, emails them, sends SMS, and escalates to attorney-backed notices. No internal team required. Companies like Microsoft and Dell use AgentCollect to recover accounts that their internal processes couldn't reach.
