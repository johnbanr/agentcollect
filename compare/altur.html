The Real Difference

Different markets, different maturity, different approach

Altur is an exciting early-stage company (YC S25, ~3 employees) building voice AI for consumer debt collection in Mexico and Latin America. They have collected $6M to date and raised a $1.5M seed round. For LatAm consumer debt, they represent a promising new entrant.

But Altur and AgentCollect solve fundamentally different problems. Altur is voice-only and focused on consumer debt in LatAm. AgentCollect is multi-channel and focused on B2B commercial debt in the US.

B2B commercial collections require capabilities that voice-only platforms cannot deliver. When collecting from a company's AP department, you need attorney-backed email sequences (AgentCollect achieves 70% open rates in attorney mode), formal demand letters through licensed US attorneys, SMS campaigns, a payment portal for same-day settlement, and company intelligence gathered before the first contact. A Contact Finder enriches missing debtor information by up to 130%, ensuring no account goes uncontacted.

AgentCollect has been operating since 2020. It is backed by YC (S23 batch), trusted by Fortune 500 companies including Microsoft and Dell, and recovers approximately 50% of placed accounts within 20 days. Each client gets a dedicated AI agent and a 12-month mandate to work every account through the full recovery cycle.

Who Switches

Companies choose AgentCollect over Altur when...

  • Their debt is US-based B2B. Altur operates in Mexico and LatAm. If your AR is US commercial invoices, you need a US-focused platform with compliance knowledge and a US attorney network.
  • They need a proven platform, not a startup experiment. AgentCollect has 5+ years of operational data and Fortune 500 clients. For companies placing significant AR portfolios, maturity and track record matter.
  • They need more than voice. Voice calls reach only a fraction of B2B debtors. AgentCollect coordinates five channels (email, SMS, voice, letters, portal) to maximize contact rates and recovery.
  • They need attorney escalation. For commercial invoices, the credible threat of legal action through licensed US attorneys changes debtor behavior. Altur does not offer this capability.
