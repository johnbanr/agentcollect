The Real Difference

Reminders vs. recovery

Chaser does one thing well: it automates payment reminder emails through tight integrations with Xero and QuickBooks. For small businesses that need to stop manually chasing invoices, it's a solid starting point.

The challenge is that payment reminders have a ceiling. After the third or fourth email, response rates drop sharply. Accounts that are truly stuck — disputed invoices, unresponsive contacts, or cash-strapped debtors — need escalation channels that Chaser doesn't offer: phone calls, legal notices, and real-time dispute resolution.

AgentCollect is built for the accounts that reminders can't close. Each account gets a dedicated AI agent that runs a full recovery sequence across voice, email, SMS, and attorney-backed notices. The result is approximately 50% recovery in 20 days — without your team lifting a finger.
