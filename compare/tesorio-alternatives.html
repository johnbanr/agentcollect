The Real Difference

Workflow automation vs. active recovery

Tesorio is a strong AR automation platform. It helps finance teams track invoices, send reminders, and forecast cash flow. For companies with an internal collections team that needs better tooling, Tesorio is a solid choice.

The gap appears when accounts go truly overdue — past 60, 90, or 120 days. At that point, automated email reminders have already been sent and ignored. What's needed is escalation: a phone call, a different tone, a legal notice. Tesorio doesn't offer voice calls or attorney escalation because that's not what it was built for.

AgentCollect picks up where workflow tools leave off. Each overdue account gets its own AI agent that researches the debtor company, identifies the right contact, and deploys a multi-channel sequence — calls, emails, SMS, and attorney-backed notices — until the account is resolved. No manual work required.

Who Switches

Companies look for Tesorio alternatives when...

  • Reminders aren't enough. Email nudges worked for the first 30 days, but a meaningful portion of accounts need a phone call or legal escalation to move.
  • The internal team can't scale. Tesorio optimizes workflow for your existing collectors — but if you don't have enough collectors, the bottleneck remains.
  • They want success-only pricing. Paying a SaaS subscription regardless of recovery results becomes hard to justify when write-offs are climbing.
