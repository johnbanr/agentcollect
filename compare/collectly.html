The Real Difference

Healthcare billing and B2B commercial debt are different worlds

Collectly is a genuinely well-designed product — for its intended market. Healthcare patient billing has its own vocabulary: EOBs, insurance coordination, patient financial assistance programs. Collectly's workflows, language, and compliance posture are calibrated for that environment.

B2B commercial debt is fundamentally different. A $120,000 invoice from a manufacturing company to a distributor involves different stakeholders (AP departments, CFOs, procurement managers), different regulatory frameworks (UCC Article 2, not FDCPA), and different negotiation dynamics. The person who owes you money is usually a professional who responds to professional escalation — including attorney letters and direct phone calls.

AgentCollect is designed exclusively around this commercial dynamic. Our voice agent knows how to navigate a gatekeeper, reach an AP manager, and present payment options in language that gets commercial accounts resolved. That specialization isn't an add-on — it's the entire product.

Who Switches

Companies switch from Collectly when…

  • They're not a healthcare company. Collectly's UX, compliance templates, and integrations are designed for patient billing. B2B commercial teams generally find themselves fighting the tool rather than using it.
  • They need attorney escalation. Commercial invoices often require a formal demand letter on attorney letterhead to move. Collectly doesn't provide this layer — AgentCollect does.
  • Monthly SaaS fees outpace results. Paying a platform subscription whether or not accounts are recovered creates pressure. AgentCollect's success-only model aligns incentives completely.
