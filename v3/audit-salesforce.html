Key Findings

critical Complete Failure to Connect Callers to Human Agents

All 10 mystery shopping calls failed to reach a live representative. Callers encountered automated systems that either didn't recognize responses or led to dead ends, resulting in 100% abandonment rate.

critical Broken Voice Recognition System

The automated phone system consistently failed to recognize caller inputs, creating frustrating loops where callers received 'Sorry. We did not recognize your response' messages repeatedly.

high Inconsistent Company Branding

Phone system answered with different company names - some calls reached 'Salesforce' while others reached 'Reliance Incorporated', indicating potential vendor confusion or system misconfiguration.

high No Accommodation for Urgent Situations

Even callers with urgent scenarios (hospitalized persona, frustrated repeat caller) could not bypass the broken automated system to reach human assistance.

medium Billing Department Redirects to Self-Service Only

When callers successfully navigated to billing (option 3), they were directed to an online portal rather than human assistance, with no option to speak to a representative.
