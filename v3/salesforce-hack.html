No self-service cancellation

There is no “Cancel Subscription” button anywhere in Salesforce. No settings page, no billing portal, no admin panel. You cannot cancel online. Period.

🔗 Salesforce Help: “Contact your Account Executive”
🔄

Auto-renewal trap

Contracts auto-renew for the full original term (usually 1–3 years) unless you send written notice 30+ days before expiration. Miss the window by one day? You’re locked in for another year.

🔗 Salesforce MSA, Section 11.2
💰

No refunds, no reductions

Fees are non-cancellable and non-refundable. If you signed for 100 seats but only use 15, you pay for 100. Salesforce does not prorate mid-term reductions.

🔗 Salesforce MSA, Section 5
🔍

salesforce.com/cancellation does not exist

We checked. No cancellation page. No billing FAQ. No self-service portal. The only path: call your AE, negotiate, and hope your contract window hasn’t closed.
