The Discovery

82% of your disputes aren't disputes.

We analyzed every single dispute across all three batches. Out of 69 disputes ($153K), 53 were invoice or proof-of-debt requests. These aren't people refusing to pay — they're AP contacts asking "can I see the invoice before I process this?"

Of those 53 who asked, 20 paid after receiving the invoice. 30 never received it at all. Your AI agent found a process gap that humans missed across years of collection effort.
