— Why this is an AR problem, not a support problem —

Drivers who want to pay can't. Drivers who want to dispute wait weeks.

Finding 01
No active payment path
A driver carrying a negative balance from a damage charge or fare adjustment cannot proactively pay it down. Uber's only mechanism is offset against future earnings.
Finding 02
Disputes route through templated email
Drivers report multi-week back-and-forth with generic responses. The agent who reviews the dispute is rarely the agent who replies. State turns over each round.
Finding 03
Churn risk on the highest-LTV partners
A driver waiting on a $340 dispute is a driver not earning. Drivers with strong unit economics have the most leverage to switch platforms while they wait.
"Negative balances are deducted from future earnings until zero. Drivers in the same area report similar issues."
— Uber Drivers Forum · uberpeople.net negative-balance thread
