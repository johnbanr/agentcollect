How It Works

Three steps to automated collection

Step 01 — Connect

Authorize HubSpot

Connect your HubSpot portal in one click via OAuth. AgentCollect reads your Deals, Contacts, Companies, and invoice properties. Works with any HubSpot tier — no paid add-ons required.

Step 02 — Configure

Set your workflow trigger

Build a HubSpot workflow that fires a webhook to AgentCollect when a deal reaches a specific stage, a contact property changes, or an invoice passes its due date. Full control over the trigger logic.

Step 03 — Collect

AI agents engage debtors

AgentCollect sends personalized emails, makes AI voice calls, and escalates to attorney-backed demand letters — under your brand. Every touchpoint logs back to HubSpot as an Engagement.

FAQ

HubSpot integration questions

Does the AgentCollect HubSpot integration require HubSpot Sales Hub?
No. AgentCollect works with any HubSpot tier — including the free CRM. You don't need Sales Hub, Service Hub, or any paid add-on to connect. The integration uses HubSpot's standard API.
Can I trigger collection from a HubSpot workflow?
Yes. You can trigger AgentCollect directly from any HubSpot workflow. When a deal reaches a specific stage, a contact property changes, or an invoice passes its due date, a webhook action calls AgentCollect's API and collection begins automatically.
Are HubSpot contacts updated when debtors respond?
Yes. When a debtor replies to an email, answers a call, or makes a payment, AgentCollect updates the corresponding HubSpot Contact and Deal record immediately. Contact properties, deal stage, and engagement timeline all reflect real-time collection activity.
Will collection emails appear in the HubSpot contact timeline?
Yes. All AgentCollect outreach — emails, call logs, letter dispatches — is synced back to HubSpot as Engagements and logged on the Contact and Deal timeline. Your team sees a complete picture without leaving HubSpot.
