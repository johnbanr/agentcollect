How It Works

Three steps to a complete AR stack

Step 01 — Connect

Export aged receivables

Send unresolved aged items from Billtrust to AgentCollect via Billtrust's API or scheduled file export. AgentCollect accepts both real-time pushes and batch uploads — no disruption to your existing Billtrust setup.

Step 02 — Configure

Define the handoff threshold

Set which accounts should move from Billtrust's digital dunning to AgentCollect's voice and legal track — by invoice age, balance size, or engagement status (e.g., no portal login after 45 days).

Step 03 — Collect

Voice and legal where digital stops

AgentCollect's AI voice agents call the debtor directly, negotiate payment, and escalate to attorney demand letters when needed — all under your brand. Outcomes sync back to Billtrust to keep your AR aging clean.

FAQ

Billtrust integration questions

Does AgentCollect replace Billtrust?
No. AgentCollect extends Billtrust. Billtrust is excellent at eInvoicing, digital payment acceptance, and automated AR workflows for B2B. AgentCollect adds what Billtrust doesn't provide: AI voice calls, human negotiation, and attorney-backed demand letters for accounts that digital-first outreach can't reach.
How does the Billtrust handoff to AgentCollect work?
When Billtrust's digital dunning workflow reaches a defined threshold — typically after a set number of days with no response or payment — aged receivables are exported to AgentCollect via Billtrust's API or a scheduled file export. AgentCollect then activates its voice and legal collection sequence.
Does AgentCollect update Billtrust when a payment is collected?
Yes. When AgentCollect collects a payment, it sends the payment confirmation and invoice update back to Billtrust via API. The invoice is marked paid in Billtrust and the aged receivable is cleared from your AR aging report.
What aged receivables should be sent from Billtrust to AgentCollect?
The best candidates are invoices that have gone unresolved for 60+ days despite Billtrust's digital outreach, accounts that have not viewed or opened invoices through Billtrust's portal, and high-balance accounts where a direct voice touchpoint is warranted.
