QuickBooks + AgentCollect

Connect QuickBooks Online and let AI agents collect your overdue invoices automatically. No CSV exports. No manual follow-ups. Payments reconcile back to QuickBooks in real time.

Setup

Live in 20 minutes

Three steps. No engineering required. No CSV files to manage.

1

Connect

Authorize AgentCollect to read your QuickBooks invoices with one click. OAuth 2.0 — we never see your QuickBooks password.

2

Configure rules

Set your overdue threshold (e.g., 30+ days), minimum amount, and customer exclusions. Choose AI Collection or Attorney Collection mode.

3

Go live

AgentCollect syncs overdue invoices daily and starts contacting debtors. Payments are automatically recorded back in QuickBooks.
