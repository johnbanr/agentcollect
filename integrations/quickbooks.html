How It Works

Three steps from connection to collected

Step 01 — Connect

OAuth in 2 minutes

Authorize AgentCollect via QuickBooks' official OAuth 2.0 flow. No passwords shared, no manual API keys. Works on all QuickBooks Online plans from Simple Start to Advanced.

Step 02 — Configure

Set your overdue threshold

Choose when invoices enter the collection workflow — 30, 45, 60, or 90 days past due. Exclude customers by tag, minimum amount, or customer type. Takes about 15 minutes to configure.

Step 03 — Collect

AI agents take it from here

AgentCollect's AI agents call, email, and text your debtors on your behalf. When payment is confirmed, the invoice status in QuickBooks updates automatically.

FAQ

QuickBooks integration FAQ

Does AgentCollect modify data in QuickBooks?
AgentCollect is read-only for invoice data. The only writes back to QuickBooks are payment records when a debtor pays through AgentCollect, and invoice status updates. We never modify the original invoice amount or terms.
Which QuickBooks versions does the integration support?
AgentCollect integrates with QuickBooks Online (all plans: Simple Start, Essentials, Plus, Advanced). QuickBooks Desktop and QuickBooks Enterprise are not currently supported.
Can I exclude certain customers from collection?
Yes. You can exclude customers by tag, customer type, invoice amount threshold, or days overdue. For example, you can set the integration to only send invoices over $500 that are more than 45 days past due.
How long does it take to set up the QuickBooks integration?
The OAuth connection takes about 2 minutes. Configuring your overdue threshold, exclusion rules, and collection workflow takes another 15–18 minutes. Most customers are live within 20 minutes of signing up.
