Purpose

This Letter of Intent sets out the commercial terms agreed between Respaid Inc. (d/b/a AgentCollect) ("AgentCollect") and CompuGroup Medical US, Inc. ("CGM") for the deployment of an AI-powered patient accounts receivable (AR) engine on the CGM Virtuox division, pending execution of a full Master Services Agreement (MSA) and Statement of Work (SOW).

Scope — Virtuox Division (Year 1)

  • Dedicated AI voice agent, English-language, Virtuox-branded caller ID.
  • Lane A: 121–365 days aged patient AR, balances $50–$2,500.
  • 3-second pickup, 24/7 availability, card-over-phone payment capture.
  • HIPAA BAA + TCPA compliance, full call recordings, consent management.
  • Human escalation path to Virtuox team: disputes, hardship, deceased.
  • Patient payment portal + SMS (Stripe, ACH, payment plans).
  • Weekly performance dashboard: reach rate, RPC, PTP, paid.
  • Weekly prompt tuning, lane expansion reviews, quarterly business reviews.

Beyond Virtuox — Phase 2 commitment. For additional CGM clients beyond Virtuox, the per-client tier will be structured together in Phase 2 based on Virtuox's actual results. Commitment: every tier will be profitable for CGM at every client size. A 5K-call/month client will not pay Virtuox-tier pricing — pricing will be calibrated to CGM's commission economics so CGM nets positive on every deployment.

Milestones

  • Milestone 1 — Commercial lock-in (by Friday, May 1, 2026, 6:00 PM PT): CGM countersigns this LOI and triggers payment of the $47,893 setup fee. Partner pricing ($47,893 setup, $13,947/mo) is locked for the full term. Engineering allocation begins Monday May 4. Procurement processing of 2-3 days post-signature for wire is acceptable.
  • Milestone 2 — Data feed & setup (CGM / AgentCollect): CGM provides the Virtuox patient AR data feed (format and cadence agreed with CGM IT). AgentCollect configures the Virtuox-branded agent, caller ID, HIPAA BAA, prompts, and escalation workflows. Monthly subscription begins on signature.
  • Milestone 3 — Go-live (target Monday June 16, 2026): AgentCollect commits to deploy and go live within 6 weeks of Milestone 1 countersignature. The 12-month term runs from signature.

Billing & Payment

  • Setup fee: $47,893 invoiced at LOI countersignature; payable via Stripe link (card or ACH) or wire transfer.
  • Monthly subscription: $13,947/mo billed monthly via Stripe, beginning on signature.
  • Term: 12 months firm, starting at LOI countersignature. Termination only if AgentCollect's AI underperforms Virtuox's 12-month recovery baseline for two (2) consecutive months, with sixty (60) day written notice. Setup fee is non-refundable.
Partner pricing — lock-in condition. CGM countersigns this LOI by Friday, May 1, 2026, 6:00 PM PT. Setup stays at $47,893 (52% off list $99,940), monthly at $13,947/mo (53% off list $29,940). After this window: pricing reverts to list, and AgentCollect's engineering allocation moves to other RCM partnerships in our pipeline. Next available kickoff: January 2027.

Multi-Client Expansion (Indicative, Phase 2)

Subject to Phase 2 refinement based on Virtuox's actual results and CGM's commission economics per client. Indicative tiers locked in this LOI:

  • Clients 2 to 5 (Virtuox-class volume, $200K+ collections/mo): $10,940/mo per client + $4,940 activation.
  • Clients 6 to 10: $8,940/mo per client + $4,940 activation.
  • 10+ clients: $79,840/mo master license, unlimited deployments.
  • Smaller clients (under $50K collections/mo) will be priced to keep CGM net positive at every deployment, calibrated to commission economics.

Binding Commercial Terms · Non-Binding Operational Details

The commercial terms set out above (pricing, fees, term, milestones, discount lock-in conditions) are binding on both parties upon countersignature of this Letter of Intent. Final operational terms (SLAs, data formats, security addenda, governance cadence) are set out in the Master Services Agreement and Statement of Work to be executed within ten (10) business days of LOI countersignature. The setup fee invoice, once paid, is non-refundable except in the case of AgentCollect's failure to begin service delivery within fourteen (14) days of countersignature.
