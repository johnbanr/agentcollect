Accounting Intelligence

"Paid Already" is a recovery,
not a miss

When a debtor pays after receiving our outreach — even if they say "I already paid" — that is a recovered account. Here is why.

Outreach-Triggered
The account was placed because it was past due. Our outreach prompted the debtor to resolve it — even if they claim it was already handled. The timing proves causation.
AR Reconciliation
These accounts were unresolved in Dell's AR system when placed. Whether the debtor paid Dell directly or through us, the outreach was the catalyst that closed the loop.
Temporal Proof
We can show the exact email, SMS, or call that preceded the payment. When payment follows outreach by hours or days, that is attribution — not coincidence.
Industry Standard
Every major collection agency counts "paid already" as recovered. The ARM industry standard is clear: if the account was placed and the debtor resolved it during the recovery period, it counts.
