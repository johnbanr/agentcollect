# LinkedIn Post 3: "We replaced a collection agency"

we replaced a collection agency that was recovering 18% of our accounts.

here's what they were doing:
- 2 generic emails
- 1 phone call (if they had a number)
- silence for 6 months
- 25-50% fee on whatever trickled in

here's what they weren't doing:
- researching the debtor before calling
- finding the right contact when the number on file was wrong
- adjusting tone based on the situation
- following up consistently for months
- offering payment plans proactively

so we switched to AI agents that do all of the above. every account gets its own agent. it researches, calls at the right time, negotiates payment plans, handles disputes.

the result:
- ~50% recovery rate (vs 18%)
- 5-15% fee (vs 25-50%)
- same-day direct payment to your account
- 0 complaints

the end of manual collection.
