Why It Matters

The Hidden Cost of Collection Fees

Most companies focus on recovery rate when evaluating a collection partner, but net recovery is the metric that matters. A 30% recovery rate with a 40% fee means you keep only 18 cents of every delinquent dollar. A 50% recovery rate with a 10% fee means you keep 45 cents. Same effort, 2.5x the result.

Traditional collection agencies built their pricing model when human collectors were the only option. Each collector handles 100-150 accounts, so agencies need high fees to cover labor costs. AI collection eliminates that constraint. One AI agent per account, unlimited accounts simultaneously, with overhead costs distributed across the platform.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is collection ROI?
Collection ROI measures the net return from your debt collection efforts after subtracting all fees and costs. It is calculated as (Amount Recovered minus Collection Fees) divided by Total Delinquent AR. A higher ROI means you are keeping more of each dollar recovered. Traditional agencies typically deliver 10-20% net ROI due to high fees (25-50%), while AI collection platforms deliver 35-45% net ROI with lower fees (5-15%) and higher recovery rates.
How much do traditional collection agencies charge?
Traditional collection agencies charge contingency fees of 25-50% of the amount recovered. The exact rate depends on the age of the debt, the volume of accounts, and the agency. First-party placements (under 90 days) typically run 25-35%, while older or smaller placements can reach 40-50%. Some agencies also charge flat fees per account ($10-25) or minimum monthly fees.
What recovery rate should I expect from collections?
Traditional collection agencies recover 20-30% of placed accounts over a 3-6 month period. AI collection platforms like AgentCollect recover approximately 50% within 20 days. The difference comes from technology: AI agents research each debtor individually, identify the right decision-maker, and execute multi-channel outreach at scale.
How does AI collection improve ROI over traditional agencies?
AI collection improves ROI in two ways: higher recovery rates (approximately 50% vs 20-30%) and lower fees (5-15% vs 25-50%). On a $1M delinquent portfolio, a traditional agency at 25% recovery and 35% fee nets you $162,500. AI collection at 50% recovery and 10% fee nets you $450,000. That is 2.8x more money back in your account from the same portfolio.
