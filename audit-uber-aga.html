The gap you described

What Uber collections doesn't do today — and what AGA can't change.

1

Email-only outreach

"Just doing emails, no calls." A phone number unanswered is a debt uncollected. 40% of our collected debts begin with a first phone call from the debtor.

2

No negotiation layer

"Transaction oriented." AGA's collectors work from a script. No promise-to-pay capture, no payment-plan logic, no empathy branches.

3

Scope limited to what they accept

Agencies cherry-pick. Accounts AGA doesn't handle fall through the cracks and never reach anyone. That's where AI-native starts.

4

Hundreds of BPOs to govern

Enrique runs service delivery across all BPOs. Every vendor has its own SLA, portal, reporting format, QBR cadence. The overhead is the cost.

5

Your brand invisible

When AGA calls, the debtor hears "this is AG Adjustments calling for Uber." With AgentCollect, the AI calls as Uber. Same legal rigor, better brand protection.

6

No exception intelligence

Your collectors only see problems after the fact. Our platform surfaces exceptions live, with the conversation context attached. That's the back-end you asked to see next.

AGA, or AgentCollect.

Not a replacement of your collectors. A replacement of the third-party layer, with the ability to extend upstream when you decide.
